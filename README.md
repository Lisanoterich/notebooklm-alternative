## What This Is

If you use [NotebookLM](https://notebooklm.google.com/) for AI-powered document Q&A but want:
- Your documents to **stay on your device** (not uploaded to Google Cloud)
- **No AI training** on your content
- **Larger knowledge bases** (20,000+ notes vs 50 sources)
- **Full rich-text editor** with Markdown, LaTeX, Mermaid diagrams
- **Offline search** capability

Then [NoteRich](https://noterich.com) may be the alternative you're looking for.

## About NoteRich

[NoteRich](https://noterich.com) is a privacy-first, AI-powered note-taking application that runs entirely in the browser. Your original notes stay 100% on your device. For AI features, only minimal, temporary context is sent to the cloud — without compromising your privacy.

- **Local-first**: Data stored in your browser (IndexedDB), not on remote servers
- **AI-powered RAG**: Ask natural language questions across all your documents
- **Rich editor**: Markdown + WYSIWYG, with LaTeX, Mermaid, video embedding
- **Cross-device**: P2P encrypted sync — no cloud intermediary
- **41 languages**: Full multilingual support for search and AI Q&A
